About

For our first class of Communications Lab, two of my classmates and I were tasked with shooting a film in 15 minutes and editing it in 15 minutes. We decided to make the film about our daily morning routine, right from when we wake up until we head to class.

This film was created in collaboration on Zoom using our smartphones and iPad cameras.

After we filmed the video, we were tasked to create a website to showcase our film to the public. The hosting website would be Github Pages. Thank you.
